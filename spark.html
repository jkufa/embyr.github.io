All-Encompassing

Our robotics platform will be a single, modular robot that will allow students to enter at any level and continue to college-level engineering. Our motto for SPARK is “teaching robotics as a solution, not a task.” We believe that to properly prepare for the world of industry, students should learn how to use robotics to solve problems, rather than treating their construction as the final goal. Within our curriculum, we intend to emphasize mechanical and electrical engineering,as well as computer science as intertwining disciplines, allowing students to learn to reach their goals, whatever skill set is required.

Extensibility

We are creating an integrated curriculum to better engage students in STEM through online, interactive modules and a single robotics platform to span the entire course. We plan to create an entry level kit with expandable modules. These modules will include a more in-depth exploration of various emphasis areas and design elements, such as electronics, custom PCB's, advanced programming concepts, and discrete logic.

Accessibility

Our goal is to bring students to a level where they can use robotics to solve problems in their own community through critical thinking and problem solving. Our spending for the original prototype was below $75, so you can be sure that our entry level kit is accessible to all budgets. The robotics platform itself will be fixed price, while the curriculum will be based on semesterly and yearly subscriptions, so you only pay for what you need.
