Who We Are

We are a group of students at Missouri University of Science and Technology bent on creating a new world, one great idea at a time.

Our Vision

A spark of inspiration can ignite change. Embyr strives to do the same. We want to ignite technological innovation from small ideas and out of the box thinking. Our motto is that we are a function that maps ideas to reality. We hope to create open source technology to help people and make lives easier. We are a group of like minded innovators who are willing to take on the tough problems. Our logo represents our out of the box thinking, hoping to spark a change.

What We Do

Through our research and technological advancement, we hope to provide our communities and the world as a whole with the platform they need to create. We believe that the best way to improve an environment is to provie the tools for others to build up the world around them, allowing them to shape the world for the better.
